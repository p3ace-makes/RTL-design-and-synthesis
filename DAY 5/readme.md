## Overview
On Day 5, various Verilog coding styles were explored to understand how they affect synthesis results in Yosys. The focus was on identifying efficient hardware implementations, avoiding unintended latch inference, and verifying functional correctness through RTL Simulation, Synthesis, and Gate-Level Simulation (GLS).
